Description

Step into this beautifully presented garden flat, thoughtfully designed with a modern aesthetic and quality finishes. This home offers three double bedrooms, two well-appointed bathrooms, and three versatile living areas, perfect for families or professionals seeking comfort and style.

The modern fitted kitchen is open plan to a generous living room, featuring solid wooden floors and integrated appliances, including a dishwasher and washing machine, for ultimate convenience. The expansive open-plan area is further enhanced by bi-folding doors that open onto a secluded private courtyard garden, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor living experience.

The principal bedroom suite occupies the first floor, with over 3 metres ceilings heights plus an additional bedroom/study with views over towards The Heath. While the second floor offers a further two/three bedrooms and a family bathroom. A spiral staircase leads to the top floor which offers extensive storage within a summer room and access to the roof terrace with exceptional views.

Both double-sized bedrooms are designed for comfort, with the family bathroom boasting a sleek three-piece suite, complete with a shower attachment over the bath. Additionally, a separate wet room provides added functionality. Walnut doors throughout the flat lend a sophisticated touch, while the property is wired for Sky TV and aerial points in the living room, kitchen, and all bedrooms.

Built just five years ago, this property remains under a 10-year building guarantee, ensuring peace of mind and quality assurance for its new owners. Whether you're entertaining guests or enjoying a quiet evening at home, this stylish and contemporary flat caters to all your needs.
